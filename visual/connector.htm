In this version you can:

  • move boxes by clicking and dragging
  • connect connectors by dropping the handles (box ends of the lines) on the connection boxes of the bigger boxes
  • connected connector lines follow when boxes are dragged around
  • disconnect connectors by dropping them in open space
Redrawing the lines while moving takes a bit of CPU power but have a look at the difference in speed between the Chrome browser and Firefox or, for a laugh, Internet Explorer of any version.
